Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add water and knead the dough until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  5. Bake the bread for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
